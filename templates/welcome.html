Caption Contest

This is part of Max Rudolph and Rishov Sarkar’s final project for ECE 8803 ODM “Online Decision Making in Machine Learning.”

You will be presented with a series of comics with captions and asked to rate each caption as a “funny,” “somewhat funny,” or “not funny” caption for that comic. After choosing a rating, you will be presented with the next comic-caption pair.

You may stop whenever you like, but we ask that if you return to the site to continue rating captions, please do so on this device. Your progress is tracked with a cookie.

Thanks for your participation!
